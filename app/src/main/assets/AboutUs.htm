About BookSagar :

BOOKSAGAR is a globally recognized and loved! - free classifieds app that lets you buy and sell new & used books locally. We bring to you a beautifully functional app with a beautiful interface that lets you find and make great deals for your Books, Magazine, Novels, encyclopedias, and other books.

  • Post a free ad with your verified phone number and attract more buyers.
  • Save precious time and money! Filter localities in your city to find sellers and buyers closest to you.
  • Manage your ads on the go - change, remove or re-activate your listings.
  • Chat privately with sellers, and negotiate prices safely.
  • Indulge in buying and selling of used books without paying any commission or transactional fee to us. Yes. It's 100% free.
  • Get real-time alerts and notifications from other buyers & sellers nearby.
  • See the most popular and trending books deals in your city.
  • Enjoy a flea-market in your hand that lets you search, spot and save ads you like. Get the best deals on used & new books.
